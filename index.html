The Beginning

A Story of Resilience

Sofy Nashipae and Martien — a partnership of purpose

Sofy Nashipae, a resilient soul from Oloolaimutia in the Maasai Mara, knows firsthand the struggle of living in poverty. In 2022, fate brought Sofy and Martien together — two dreamers who shared a vision of cultural connection and community upliftment.

"Together, their efforts blossomed into a beacon of success: a sanctuary of culture, community, and connection."

By November 2024, their dream took physical form — Nashipae Cultural Oasis, a luxury homestay built in the Kenyan savanna. Western comfort meets 100% Maasai authenticity. Owned and operated by Maasai, every guest's stay directly benefits local families.

But Sofy's aspirations extend far beyond the walls of her oasis. With a heart as vast as the Maasai Mara itself, she dreams of creating a legacy that empowers others to break free from poverty and embrace a future filled with promise.

What Guests Experienced

Unforgettable Moments

From epic safaris to intimate cultural encounters, every day brought a new adventure that guests carried with them forever.

Adventure

Maasai Mara Safari

Wake before sunrise and head straight into the Maasai Mara game reserve. Seasoned guides who grew up in the area ensure you never miss a moment of wildlife action.

Culture

Maasai Village Tour

Walk a few hundred meters and step into another world — Maasai people living traditionally. Experience the famous jumping greeting, folk dances, bead making, and ancient fire-starting techniques.

Cuisine

Maasai Barbecue

Nyama Choma prepared the traditional way. Maasai men show the full process while sharing customs around the campfire — an authentic celebration of culture and food.

Craft

Maasai Beadmaking

Learn the centuries-old art of Maasai beaded jewellery. Colourful necklaces, bracelets, and pendants — each piece tells a story and supports local artisans.

Adventure

Balloon Safari

Soar above the Great Wildebeest Migration and witness the Maasai Mara from a perspective few ever experience. A once-in-a-lifetime moment floating above the savanna.

Cuisine

Authentic Kenyan Dining

From traditional Ugali and Managu to homemade pizza and fresh smoothies — our kitchen served both Kenyan soul food and international comfort dishes.
